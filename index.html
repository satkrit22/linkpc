WHAT IS LINK PC & LINK PC +

A thin client device is a lightweight computer designed to connect to a server for virtual computing. Instead of handling intensive tasks locally, it relies on a central server to manage processing, storage, and applications. Thin clients are commonly used in environments where centralized management and resource efficiency are crucial, such as in businesses, educational institutions, and remote work setups.
